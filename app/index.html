Underground Theatre

Car Directions

The best way is to get a taxi and ask the driver to deliver you to the Underground Theatre at VGUES (It’s a short russian name for Vladivostok State University of Economics and Service). You can visit the theatre by your car, but you have to have enough time to find a parking lot.

Walking Directions

Find VGUES (It’s a short russian name for Vladivostok State University of Economics and Service / VSUES) on the map and go to the building. If the road behind you and you faced to the main entrance of the building, walk to the left entrance with big “Underground” sign. Enter the left entrance and it’s straight on the first floor.
